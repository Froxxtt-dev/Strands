How to Care for Your Wigs

Daily maintenance
Gently brush with a wide-tooth comb from ends to roots. Store on a wig stand or mannequin head when not wearing.
Washing instructions
Use lukewarm water and a sulfate-free shampoo made for human hair. Rinse thoroughly and air dry only. Never use a hair dryer or direct heat.
Styling tips
Heat-safe up to 180°C. Always use heat protectant spray. Avoid sleeping in the wig to prevent tangling.
Storage & longevity
Keep away from direct sunlight and dust. Use a silk or satin bag for travel. Proper care can make your wig last 6–12 months or more.
What if my rental wig gets damaged?
Minor tangling is normal. Major damage may result in losing part or all of the refundable deposit.
